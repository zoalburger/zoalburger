Overview

The module brings an option to add add-on(extension) cost of the products in the point of sale. You can configure the add-on as a product, so it brings all the product features to the add-on. It is simple to handle by choosing the add-on from the sidebar menu not affecting your product screen. It displays the add-on cost in the receipt too.

Features

Option to set add-ons with its price, unit of measure, etc

Add add-on to the corresponding product by simply clicking from the side menu.

Shows the add-on cost in the receipt

Simply handle the add-on and its configuration from the backend menu

Screenshots

Select the Addons Product

Enable the 'Is Add-on' in the product configuration form which uses add-on product.

Adding the Addons Product

Enable the 'Has Add-on' in the product form -> Sales tab and choose the add-on products.

Product Addons on Sidebar

Click on a product which has the add-on items in the point of sale screen. It will show a sidebar menu with all the add-on items.

Adding the Addons Product

Click on any add-on in order to add it under the product in the order line.

Addons On Receipt

The add-on and its price will be shown in the receipt.
